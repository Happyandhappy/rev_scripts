  • Are you confined to a bed, a wheelchair, or are you using a cane or walker?
  • YES:  If your house was on fire you could get out without it?
  • CANE: Do you mostly use the cane on longer walks and things like that?

  • Are you in a nursing home, hospital, or do you receive hospice or home health care? Do you require assistance to feed, bathe and dress yourself?
  • NOTE: If receiving home health care, find out if the person is just popping in and cleaning and preparing meals, or if they need assistance with feeding, bathing, or dressing themselves. (this is mostly for LBL disability)
  • NOTE: If they are currently in the hospital, long-term care, assisted living, or hospice - KO for all companies.

  • Do you have any breathing disorders like COPD, emphysema, chronic  bronchitis or do you use oxygen?
  • Chronic means that it never goes away. Not seasonal.
  • BL - If they can answer all the following correctly, then SGI. If not, SGII
  1. When were you first diagnosed? (Needs to be more than 2 yrs.)
  2. Do you use a nebulizer? (needs to be no)
  3. Have you seen your doctor specifically about COPD in the past 2 years? (needs to be no)

MEDICATIONS:


  • Have you been asked to stop using drugs or alcohol, or have you been treated for abuse?
  • YES:   Have you actually been treated* for abuse, like hospitalized or rehab treatment?   *AA is not considered treatment.

  • Have you been convicted of a felony or incarcerated, or are you on parole or probation or have you been refused coverage?
  • What were you convicted of?   (might be ok if convicted of white collar crime)

  • Have you been medically diagnosed  with Alzheimer's, Dementia, or memory-loss or do you take any kind of memory pill?
  • Actually been diagnosed  by a doctor with Alzheimer's or Dementia   LBL is the only company that asks the memory-loss question, and they are referring to permanent memory problems, not a temporary condition from an accident or something like that.
  • Alzheimer's and Dementia are a KO  for all companies. Make sure they have been medically diagnosed.

  • AIDS or HIV? What about Systemic Lupus or any immune disorder? Rheumatoid or Osteo-Arthritis?
  • BL considers Rheumatoid Arthritis as an immune disorder - KO

  • MS, Muscular Dystrophy, Parkinson's or ALS (Lou Gehrig's disease)? Epilepsy?
  • How do you treat your MS?
  • How do you treat your Parkinsons?
  • How do you treat your Epilepsy?

  • Any internal cancer, skin cancer excluding Basal or Sickle Cell Anemia?
  • How long have you been cancer free?
  • Have you had two or more occurrences in your lifetime?
  • How are you treating it?
  • Have you been given less than 12 months to live?  If given less than 12 months to live/terminal - KO

  • Hepatitis C, or any Liver or Kidney Disease?
  • Are you currently treating or are you cured of your Hepatitis C?
  • What stage is your Kidney Failure?

  • Have you ever had an aneurysm, stroke or a TIA, which is a mini stroke?
  • When did you have your stroke?   Over two years - Level with any company.
  • Are you affected by the stroke in any way?   If so, avoid LBL unless heat stroke
  • Are you taking blood thinners?
    Be aware of blood thinners in combo with beta blockers KO with RNA

  • Have you ever had a Heart Attack? Have you ever been diagnosed with Angina. Congestive heart failure? Irregular heartbeat? Had a pacemaker or defibrillator installed, or any type of Heart Problems?
  • If they have a pacemaker:   When was your pacemaker installed?
  • If they’ve had a heart attack:   How long ago was your heart attack? Have you ever taken Nitro tablets? When was it last prescribed?
  • BL - SGI if nitro has been prescribed but not taken AND all other questions are answered no,OR SGIII if answering yes. LBL - offers MWL
  • Do you take blood thinners?

MEDICATIONS:


  • Do you have Diabetes?
  • Is your Diabetes under control? (take your meds and see your doc regularly)
  • Are you on insulin? How old were you when you were put on insulin?
  • Do you have any complications with your diabetes, like neuropathy, or eye or kidney problems?
  • Have you ever had an amputation due to your diabetes?

  • Do you have High Blood Pressure?
  • Is your blood pressure under control?
  • What medications are you taking for your high blood pressure?
    Keep in mind many heart meds are used to treat HTN especially if heart condition was several years ago. It is always best if they are using the meds for HTN.

  • Do you take medication for anxiety or depression, or have you been hospitalized for major depression in the past two years?
  • Have you ever attempted suicide?
  • Have you ever been hospitalized due to depression?

  • Have you been hospitalized in the past 12 months for any reason?   Only LBL asks this question.
  • What were you hospitalized for?   If it was for something like broken ankle or back surgery, they need to be fully recovered.
  • Have you been released by your doctor?

  • Do you have any outstanding or pending diagnostic* testing? What about any upcoming surgeries or have you been told you need surgery?   *diagnostic does not include routine testing - ex: diabetes monitoring. Means being diagnosed for a new medical condition.

  • Are there any other prescription medications you are taking that we have not discussed?   Go over every medication they are taking. Ask about medications they have been prescribed, but may or may not still be taking. Find out when they last filled each prescription. *Refer to each company's med list regarding each drug.
